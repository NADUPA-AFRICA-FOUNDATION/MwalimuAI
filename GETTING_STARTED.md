# Mwalimu AI - Quick Start Guide

## 📱 Landing Page
Visit the public landing page at `/` to learn about the platform and sign up.

## 🚀 Quick Setup

### 1. Sign Up / Login
- Go to `/auth/sign-up` to create a new account
- Or `/auth/login` if you already have an account
- Confirm your email (in production mode)

### 2. Complete Needs Assessment
- After first login, head to `/dashboard/assessment`
- Answer questions about your CBC knowledge and challenges
- This personalizes your learning recommendations

### 3. Explore Learning Modules
- Visit `/dashboard/modules` to see all available courses
- Start with "CBC Fundamentals" for beginners
- Progress is automatically tracked

### 4. Chat with AI Coach
- Open `/dashboard/ai-coach` to start chatting
- Ask questions about teaching strategies
- Get personalized guidance in real-time

### 5. Join Community
- Head to `/dashboard/community` to connect with other teachers
- Share experiences and learn from peers
- Browse discussions by topic

## 🎯 Key Features at a Glance

### Dashboard Overview (`/dashboard`)
- See your progress at a glance
- Quick access to all main features
- Track achievements and badges

### Learning Modules (`/dashboard/modules`)
- **Difficulty Levels**: Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced
- **Categories**: CBC Fundamentals, Assessment, Pedagogy, Technology
- **Progress Tracking**: See completion % for each module
- **Flexible Pacing**: Learn at your own speed

### AI Coach (`/dashboard/ai-coach`)
- Type your questions anytime
- Get instant, personalized responses
- Suggested questions to get started
- Chat history for reference

### Needs Assessment (`/dashboard/assessment`)
- 7-step questionnaire
- Multiple question types (scale, radio, checkbox)
- Results inform your learning path
- Can be retaken anytime

### Community Forum (`/dashboard/community`)
- Filter by category (Assessment, Implementation, Technology, etc.)
- Search discussions
- View reply counts and engagement
- Create new discussions

### Resources (`/dashboard/resources`)
- Download guides, toolkits, templates
- Video content and tutorials
- Organized by topic tags
- Free access for all registered teachers

### Achievements (`/dashboard/achievements`)
- Earn badges for milestones
- Track your progress with achievements
- Share accomplishments with community
- Motivating gamification

### Settings (`/dashboard/settings`)
- Update profile information
- Manage notification preferences
- Change password
- Download your data

## 🔑 Keyboard Shortcuts (AI Coach)

- **Enter**: Send message
- **Shift + Enter**: New line in message
- **Escape**: Close suggestions (if any)

## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: How often should I log in?
**A:** As often as works for your schedule! Even 15 minutes weekly helps with learning consistency.

### Q: Can I download my learning materials?
**A**: Yes! Visit `/dashboard/resources` to download guides, templates, and toolkits.

### Q: How is my privacy protected?
**A**: All your data is encrypted and protected by Row Level Security policies. Only you can access your profile and progress data.

### Q: Can I share resources with colleagues?
**A**: You can discuss and share insights in the community forum at `/dashboard/community`.

### Q: How do I reset my password?
**A**: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, or update it in `/dashboard/settings`.
